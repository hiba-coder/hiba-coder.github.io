June 2024

Summer School

I attended a summer school on Green IT. The aim of this Summer School is to bring together leading scientific experts in Green IT and responsible digital practices to share the latest knowledge and best practices with those who are shaping tomorrow's responsible digital world. It invites experts (easyvirt, greenspector, etc) in responsible digital practices, energy efficiency (both hardware and software), data centres and cloud computing. This school helped me to expand my knowledge of Green IT concepts and ecosystems. I discovered projects aimed at detecting what they call «code smells» and proposing «nature-friendly code». The program of this school was partically oriented, allowing us to enjoy hands-on sessions where we practised «Green Coding».
